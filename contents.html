Regression model:
y_k = b_0 + b_1 t_k + x_k,
in particular, noise x_k could fit an AR(1) model:
x_k = a x_{k - 1} + sqrt(1 - a^2) e_k,
where e_k is a white noise.

Results:

  1. Exact values of coverage factor K for normal e_k

  2. Values of coverage factor K for e_k having two-side power (TSP) distribution (obtained by Monte Carlo simulation)

  3. Values of coverage factor K when x_k is 1/f^a noise (obtained by Monte Carlo simulation)
